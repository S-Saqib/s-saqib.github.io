Thank you for visting!

I am Shadman Saqib Eusuf, currently a Ph.D. student (Computer Science) in the Siebel School of Computing and Data Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I have research interests in Database Systems and Spatial Analytics. I am also interested in Smart Embedded Systems, IoT and Wireless Communication.

Prior to joining UIUC, I was a Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) in Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) for four and a half years. I completed my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering degree from the same department under the supervision of Professor Mohammed Eunus Ali. There I worked on trajectory indexing for faster query processing in large spatial and spatio-temporal databases. Outside my work, I enjoy traveling and playing video games.
